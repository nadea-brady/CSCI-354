Monster Hunter World Web App Proposal

Problem Description

Monster Hunter World presents a unique challenge to players who must gather information about monsters, resources, and gear. Currently, the information is scattered across various websites, and it’s difficult for players to keep track of everything they need to optimize their hunts. Existing websites, while helpful, are not interactive enough and do not provide tailored suggestions based on the player’s current gear and upcoming monster.

A web app that consolidates this information and provides real-time recommendations for players based on their loadouts and the monsters they are hunting will streamline the preparation process, improving gameplay efficiency and player satisfaction.

App Description

The proposed web application is designed to help Monster Hunter World players optimize their gear and plan hunts more effectively. Users will select a monster they wish to hunt, and the app will provide information on the monster’s weaknesses and suggest ideal equipment based on the player's current gear setup.

Players can input their current weapon, armor, and items, and the app will generate suggestions for optimal loadouts, taking into account the selected monster’s attributes. This feature will save time by eliminating the need to consult multiple external resources. In addition, users will have access to helpful tips, drop rates, and crafting materials needed for future hunts.

App Functionality

  1. Select a Monster: The user will first select a monster they wish to hunt. The app will then display detailed information about the monster, including weaknesses, resistances, and behaviors.
  2. Input Gear Information: The user will input their current gear setup, including weapons, armor, and items.
  3. Receive Loadout Suggestions: Based on the monster's weaknesses and the user’s input, the app will suggest the most optimal loadout for a successful hunt.

Data and Images

The web application will require data on monster attributes, including weaknesses, resistances, and drop rates. This data will be stored in a database and retrieved as users make their selections. Additional data, such as weapon stats and crafting materials, will also be available.

The app will feature images of monsters, weapons, and gear to visually engage users. These images will be sourced from external resources such as:

  • Monster Hunter World Wiki
  • Official Monster Hunter World Website
